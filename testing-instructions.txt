1. Install the app and open it inside Shopify Admin.
2. Go to `Size Charts` and create a chart manually or by CSV/paste import.
3. Go to `Assignments` and assign the chart to a product.
4. Optional: add a fallback rule in `Keyword Rules`.
5. On the `Home` page, use `Preview Matched Chart` to confirm the expected chart is selected.
6. Go to `Online Store > Themes > Customize`.
7. Open a product template and add the `Lemon Size` app block.
8. Save the theme and open the assigned product page on the storefront.
9. Click the size guide button and confirm the modal opens with the correct chart.

Please verify:
- The button appears only on products with a matching chart
- Unit switching works for supported measurement columns
- Guide image appears only when enabled
- Analytics records a size guide open

Important:
- Lemon Size uses a theme app block on the product template
- The app should be tested as an app block, not under `App embeds`
- No customer login is required
